Tribute-to-Sundarpichai

Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and computer engineer who is the CEO of Google and its parent company, Alphabet Inc. Pichai was born in Chennai, India, in 1972, and went on to study engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur before obtaining a master's degree in materials science and engineering from Stanford University. Pichai joined Google in 2004 and worked on various projects, including the development of the Chrome browser, before becoming the CEO of Google in 2015.Under Pichai's leadership, Google has expanded its product offerings to include hardware such as the Pixel smartphone and Nest smart home devices, as well as artificial intelligence initiatives like Google Assistant and Google Cloud. In addition to his work at Google, Pichai has also been an advocate for expanding access to technology and computer science education, particularly for underprivileged communities.
